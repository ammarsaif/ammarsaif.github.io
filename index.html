Coffee Company

Welcome to Coffee Company! At our cafe, we are more than just a coffee shop; we are a community of coffee enthusiasts dedicated to providing a delightful and enriching coffee experience. Nestled in the heart of Helsinki, our cafe is a haven for those seeking a moment of respite, a friendly conversation, and, of course, exceptional coffee.

Espresso

Indulge in our exquisite espresso offerings, meticulously crafted for aficionados seeking an unparalleled coffee experience. Immerse yourself in the rich, aromatic depths of our specialty espresso blends, a true delight.

Cappuccino

Savor the velvety allure of our cappuccino, where perfectly steamed milk dances with robust espresso. A harmonious balance of flavors, creating a delightful, frothy masterpiece for your enjoyment.

Ice Coffee

Quench your thirst with our refreshing iced coffee, a chilled symphony of bold espresso poured over ice and subtly sweetened. A cool, invigorating treat for warm days and coffee enthusiasts.
